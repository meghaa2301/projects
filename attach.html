Attached Zipper Bags (MINIGRIP® Bags) / MAGIC SEAL® Bags


These bags are made of monolayer or Multilayer PE / PP / PEPP.
These bags have a zipper profile separately attached to the bag to make it a re-closable bag and differentiate the bag from the regular packing bags.
The unique feature of zipper adds value to the packaging of the item and also makes it reusable and long lasting for multiple use.
These bags can be manufactured in plain as well as can be printed upto 8 colours.


Applications of Attached Zipper :
  • Garment Packaging like Shirts / Tshirts / Trousers / Denims ( Jeans ) / Leggings / Ladies dresses / vests / lingerie / etc….
  • Pharmaceutical industries
  • Food packaging industries
  • Sports Accessories industries
  • Hygiene industry
  • Cable tie industry

PP Attached Zipper Bags

PP attached Zipper bags are made out of Polypropylene (PP) and thus have a High clarity and shine on the bag with a zipper attached to the bag giving it the re-closability feature.


PP Attached Zipper bags are used in a variety of Industry applications for packing their items where the customer requires the aesthetic appeal and reusability.



PE / PEPP Attached Zipper Bags

PEPP Attached Zipper bags are made out of Polyethylene (PE) but have better clarity and shinning then the regular LDPE bags. They are also called as Super LD bags specially used in Garment packaging.


These bags are used for packaging items where the customer is looking for LDPE bags with zipper (re-closability) but with high clarity and shine to ensure the product is clearly seen when packed inside such bags.
