Retail Pack


Retail packs have been designed after receiving customer feedback for the desire to offer smaller pack quantities to cater to various packaging needs. It also ensures easy reach to every house and office.


These retail consumer packs are designed to suit the requirements of users who can use this product for different purposes. These retail packs can be used for storing Fruits & Vegetables, Sandwiches, Snacks, Cosmetics, Dry Fruits, Travel Kits, Documents, Jewellery, Stationary, medicines and many other different day to day household / office items.

Slide-Rite® - PE Storage Bags

Slide-Rite® - PE Storage bags are Multi-purpose Storage Slider bags which are re-closable bags and can be ideally used for packing fruits, vegetables, Travel Kits, Documents, Jewellery, Stationery, medicines and many other different day to day household products.


Slide-Rite® - PE Storage bags comes in 3 different size packs of Small / Medium and Large Sizes with 15 pieces in each pack. Slider runner on the Zipper profile closing makes it easy for user to open and close the bag by sliding/moving the slider back and forth on the zipper track.


These bags are made out of Polyethylene (PE).


Polygrip® – PE Colour Zipper Bags

Polygrip® – PE Colour Zipper bags are in three different Sizes as mentioned below and ideal for storage of Fruits, vegetables, Sandwiches, Travel Kits, Documents, Jewellery, Stationary, medicines and many other different day to day household products.


These bags are made out of Polyethylene (PE) and are re-usable bags and have a press to close zipper on the top for opening and closing the bag. The user can hold the zipper lock on one end of the bag with their fingers and slide their finger on the zipper lock from one end to other end to ensure the zipper bag is locked properly and bag opening is closed.


Slide-Rite® – PP Multipurpose Bags

Slide-Rite® – PP Multipurpose bags (Assorted Sizes) are a mix of 4 different Sizes of Slider bags with 5 pieces of each size and can be ideally used for packing Stationery, Cosmetics, Passport, Travel Kits and other different day to day office and household products.


This is an ideal product for household / office usage as it’s a mix of assorted sizes for different usage and different sizes item packing. It’s a value for money product since you get 4 sizes in one pack.


Slider runner on the Zipper profile closing makes it easy for user to open and close the bag by sliding/moving the slider back and forth on the zipper track These bags are made out of Polypropylene (PP).


Slide-Rite® PP Document Bags

Slide-Rite® PP Document bags are a pack of 10 Slider bags which are ideal for storage of documents, certificates, important papers, Stationery and other office purpose / Household items storage.


Slider runner on the Zipper profile closing makes it easy for user to open and close the bag by sliding/moving the slider back and forth.


These bags are made out of Polypropylene (PP).

Polygrip® – PE Zipper Bags

Polygrip® – PE Zipper bags are ideal re-closable zipper bags for day to day applications and storage of small items and can be used in household as well as in Office.


There are 4 different size packs for this item for different usage with different quantities as mentioned below in each pack.


These bags are made out of Polyethylene (PE) and re-usable bags and have a press to close zipper on the top for opening and closing the bag. The user can hold the zipper lock on one end of the bag with their fingers and slide their finger on the zipper lock from one end to other end to ensure the zipper bag is locked properly and bag opening is closed.
