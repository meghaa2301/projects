About Us

Established in 1981 in a modest way, SVP introduced the concept of Reclosable Packaging in India through its brand MAGIC SEAL®. SVP has maintained its leadership over 4 decades through a single, simple principle, “SATISFACTION OF EVERY CUSTOMER”. The credit for our market leadership goes largely to the minute attention to details and consistent R & D.


With a vision and commitment to attain reclosability on a wider range, SVP introduced the concept of Zipper Profiles and Zipper Attached Bags in India in 2003. With a firm belief in the reclosable concept, SVP continues to provide “Reclosable Packaging Products and Solutions” that are tailor-made as per customer requirements.


In our endeaveour to reach customers and their everyday needs, we have also introduced Retail packs by the brand names of Polygrip® (Zipper bags) and Slide-Rite® (Slider bags) in India.


In order to implement a systematic way of working and continually improve the processes and in turn increasing the customer satisfaction the company has implemented ISO 9001 Quality Management Systems since October 2013.


Today, SVP is the leading zipper packaging company with largest marketshare in India and also with its increasing exports it has global customer presence in different countries as well.
