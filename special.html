Speciality Zipper Bags


Zipper bags ( ZIP LOCK® bags / SELF LOCK® bags ) which are made out of Low density polyethylene ( LDPE ) with an inbuilt press to close zipper profile on the bag to make the bag re-closable.


Below are the range of Speciality Zipper Bags which we manufacture for different Applications to serve different industries, thus creating a specialised packaging along with reclosable & reusable feature.



---Anti-Static Zipper Bags
---Bubble Zipper Bags
---Coloured Zip – Zipper Bags

--- Grape Slider Bags

---Corrosion Resitant Zipper Bags
--- Oxo-Biodegradable Zipper Bag

Anti-Static Zipper Bags

Anti Static zipper bag are ziplock bags with Anti Statis additive and are thus used for storing electronic components and parts which could be damaged due to Electrostatic discharge. These bags are also called as ESD Zipper bags

Bubble Zipper Bags

These bags are attached Zipper bags with bubble wrap film on both the sides of the bags to make it a protective zipper bag with a re-closability feature.


These bags are used to pack fragile materials which require protective packing to ensure it doesn’t break or are not damaged due to impact as the bubble wrap film protects the same.

Coloured Zip – Zipper Bags

Ziplock bags with a coloured zipper locking profile as per the requirement of the customer.

Coloured Zipper Bags

Ziplock bags with coloured film as per the requirement of the customer. These colours could be Mily white, Yellow, black, etc…

Garment Bags

Attached Zipper bags which are specially designed for garment packaging.
These are made out of PP or PEPP material as per customer’s requirement.
These bags are used to pack different garments like Shirts / Tshirts / Trousers / Denims ( Jeans ) / Leggings / Ladies dresses / vests / lingerie / etc….

Hanger Zipper Bags

Attached Zipper bags which have a hanger welded on top of the flap above the zipper lock to make the product hang on a rack. These bags are majorly used for garment packaging like Vests / lingerie / Socks / Night suits / etc……

Grape Slider Bags

Slider Zipper bags with holes and cuts on the film specially designed to pack grapes in it. It also has a D-cut to make it easy to handle and carry.
These bags are widely used for grapes packaging as well as in exports of grapes.
These are specially designed bags and there is special Handi ( round bottom ) shape to the bag on the bottom which is its unique design.

Kangaroo Zipper Bags (Bio-Hazard Zipper Bags )

These are ziplock bags with a front pocket film which is welded in front of the zipper bags.
These bags with a front pocket are widely used by the Health industry (Testing laboratories) and the Hospitals.
These bags are also called as Biohazard bags / biohazard zipper bags / biohazard ziplock bags / specimen bags / specimen zipper bags.

Corrosion Resitant Zipper Bags

Available in all the sizes.

Oxo-Biodegradable Zipper Bag

Degradation initiates in presence of sunlight and oxygen.. Can be made in all sizes. Will degrade within 6 months to 2 years, as per customers requirement.
