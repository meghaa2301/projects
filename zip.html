Zipper Profiles / MAGIC SEAL® Zipper


SVP offers a variety of Zipper profiles manufactured in polyethylene and polypropylene suitable for PE/CPP & OPP films and laminates ranging from 3mm to 40mm in width.


The co-extruded zippers are made from advanced polyolefin & speciality polymers to suit various applications, laminates & converting machines to achieve maximum productivity and efficiency.


We DESIGN & DEVELOP ZIPPERS as per customer’s requirement.

---Double Flange Zipper
--- Single Flange Zipper
---Hinge Zipper
--- String Zipper
--- Powder Proof Zipper
--- Slider Zippper
--- Membrance Zippper
--- Dual Lock Zippper

Double Flange Zipper

Having Flange on both side of the zipper. Equal or unequal.

Single Flange Zipper

Having Flange on one side of zipper.

Hinge Zipper

Having one side of zipper profile as a hinge

String Zipper

Zipper with no Flanges.

Powder Proof Zipper

Zipper having broad distant profile.

Slider Zipper

Zippers with or without flange to facilitated sliding of slider.

Membrane Zipper

Zipper with one side of the zipper flange joint together.

Dual Lock Zipper

Zipper having two profiles
